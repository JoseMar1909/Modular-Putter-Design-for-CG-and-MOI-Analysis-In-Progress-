# Modular-Putter-Design-for-CG-and-MOI-Analysis-In-Progress-
Design, prototyping, and testing of a modular putter system to study center of gravity (CG), moment of inertia (MOI), and shaft configuration effects.
This project focuses on the design, prototyping, and testing of a modular golf putter system to analyze how mass distribution and shaft placement affect stability, face rotation, and impact behavior.

The design incorporates interchangeable weights and multiple shaft configurations to enable controlled experimental evaluation of center of gravity (CG) and moment of inertia (MOI).
